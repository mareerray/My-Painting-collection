❉Maire's Painting Collection❉

These are the collection of my watercolor paintings that I had learned in one month. Although painting is so satisfying and therapeutic, making a good piece of artwork is not at all as easy as it seems. My experience with watercolors especially makes me realize that 'balance' is the key. Sometimes you have to loosen your need to control and let colors and water do their magic. So, painting has become my de-stress hobby as well as a creative workshop where I can revisit as often as I'd like.
